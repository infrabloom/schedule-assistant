## How to use this template

1. Initialize at end of Phase 1 with everything from the Kickoff Meeting open question dump.
2. Update at every schedule version increment.
3. Walk PM through it at every CHECKPOINT.
4. Status flow: Open → Answered → Closed (or Deferred if not yet needed).
5. Deliverable in Phase 7 final package.
